Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Servings:

1

    1 cup strawberries
    0.5 frozen banana
    1 cup almond milk
    1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
    1.5 cups ice

    Step 1

    Combine the strawberries, banana, almond milk, honey or maple, and ice in a blender. Blend until smooth.

    Step 2

    Taste. If it's too tart for you, add another 1/2 cup or so of almond milk and another tablespoon of honey or maple.
